my story

I’m innately infatuated with the craft and science of visual communication. I want to work in it until I have severe arthritis. Which won’t matter, because I’ll probably be able to mind–control my Creative Suite by then anyways.

My formal score board current holds a degree in Linguistics and Psychology, a Bachelors in Education, and am working to add a degree in Graphic Design. You can confidently say I’m a lifer in the learning and ambition category.

I originally created Ash Tastic to prevent my students from finding me on Facebook. And, since Facebook rules the world, I’ve been known as Ash Tastic ever since. But, from that, I am always mastering, have mean talent, and produce designs people can really chew on. I am Mastic Tastic.
